Code & Craft

Weekly space to play with technology, for everyone.

Whatever your skills, this is for you.

Join us for a weekly co-working session aimed at everyone - regardless of your skills, background or circumstance. Code & Craft is an opportunity to get support on your technical projects from other attendees and the Hacksmiths team.

Whether you want to learn about web development, a specific programming language, electronics using our brand new Hardware Lab, setting up an online business, or something else, this is a space for you to let your creative juices flow. You can work in a team or alone, for a single session or over the course of many, to learn new skills, build awesome projects and share with others.

We're also a registered Code Club and Coder Dojo, which means we have access to all of their great resources aimed at young people between 7 and 17.

You don't need any skills to attend - just bring a laptop (ideally). By attending you agree to abide by our Code of Conduct. You do not need to sign up, but please contact Laila Majeed at lmaje002@gold.ac.uk if you have any questions.

Our weekly session normally takes place in Margaret McMillan Building Room 220, but is located in St James Block 3 on the following dates to facilitate trips from local colleges - 17 Jan, 28 Feb, 14 March and 28 March.
