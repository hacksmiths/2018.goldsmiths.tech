Code & Craft

Weekly space to play with technology, for everyone.

Whatever your skills, this is for you.

Join us for a free weekly making session. Whatever your age, background or skill sets, use Code & Craft to learn more and get support from people around you.

As part of Code & Craft, we run a Code Club and Coder Dojo, which come packed with resources to help you get started and improve your skill. We also have a Hardware Lab of electronics for you to use.

You can work in a team or alone, for a single session or over the course of many, to learn new skills, build awesome projects and share with others.

You don't need any skills to attend - just bring a laptop (ideally). By attending you agree to abide by our Code of Conduct.

You do not need to sign up, but please contact Laila Majeed at lmaje002@gold.ac.uk if you have any question.
